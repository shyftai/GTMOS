# Sending Calendar — Global Holidays & Blackout Dates

Before shipping a campaign, check this calendar against your target geography. Do not send cold outreach on major holidays — it tanks open rates and looks tone-deaf.

**Rule:** No cold sends on the day of or the day after a major holiday. For multi-day holidays (Christmas week, Chinese New Year, Eid), pause for the full period.

## Universal blackout periods

These apply regardless of geography:

| Period | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| Dec 23 – Jan 2 | Year-end holidays — almost nobody reads cold email |
| Aug 1 – Aug 31 (Europe) | Summer holiday season — expect 30-50% lower engagement. Consider pausing or reducing volume. |
| Week between Christmas and New Year | Dead zone worldwide |

---

## How to use

### Blackout logic (per-contact, not per-campaign)
Holidays are checked per contact based on their location, not the campaign's target geography. If your list includes US and UK contacts:
- On July 4th: pause US contacts only — UK contacts still receive emails
- On Boxing Day: pause UK contacts only — other countries still receive emails

During `/gtm:ship` and `/gtm:validate-list`, check each contact's country against this calendar. Flag contacts whose send date falls on their local holiday.

### Holiday hooks (personalization opportunity)
Holidays coming up in the next 7-14 days can be used as personalization:
- "Before the long weekend..." (before a Monday holiday)
- "Figured I'd reach out before [holiday] — things tend to go quiet after"
- "Hope you enjoyed [holiday] — quick thought on [topic]" (day after)

This works especially well for cultural holidays the prospect's peers would recognize. Use this naturally — don't force it.

### Rules
1. **On the day of a holiday:** do not send to contacts in that country — period
2. **Day before a holiday:** sending is fine, can use the holiday as a personalization hook
3. **Day after a holiday:** sending is fine, can reference the holiday naturally
4. **Multi-day holidays (Christmas, Chinese New Year, Golden Week):** pause for the full blackout window for contacts in that country
5. **Other countries:** unaffected — continue sending normally
6. Log any manual overrides in campaign logs/decisions.md
