# Cold Outbound Benchmarks — Reference

Use these benchmarks to evaluate campaign performance in health checks and debriefs.
These are industry averages for B2B cold outbound — top performers exceed these.

---

## Email metrics

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Open rate | <30% | 30-45% | 45-60% | >60% |
| Reply rate | <1% | 1-3% | 3-5% | >5% |
| Positive reply rate (of replies) | <30% | 30-45% | 45-60% | >60% |
| Bounce rate | >5% | 3-5% | 1-3% | <1% |
| Unsubscribe rate | >1% | 0.5-1% | 0.1-0.5% | <0.1% |
| Spam complaint rate | >0.3% | 0.1-0.3% | 0.05-0.1% | <0.05% |

## LinkedIn metrics

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Connection acceptance rate | <15% | 15-25% | 25-40% | >40% |
| Reply rate (after connect) | <5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | >20% |
| InMail response rate | <5% | 5-10% | 10-15% | >15% |

## Funnel conversion rates

| Stage transition | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|-----------------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| Contacted → Reply | <1% | 1-3% | 3-5% | >5% |
| Reply → Meeting booked | <20% | 20-40% | 40-60% | >60% |
| Meeting → Qualified | <30% | 30-50% | 50-70% | >70% |
| Qualified → Won | <10% | 10-25% | 25-40% | >40% |
| Full funnel (list → won) | <0.2% | 0.2-0.5% | 0.5-2% | >2% |

## Sequence performance

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Best performing touch | Usually touch 1 or 3 |
| Optimal sequence length | 3-5 touches |
| Optimal time between touches | 3-5 days |
| Optimal send time | Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am recipient timezone |
| Optimal send day | Tuesday has highest open rates, Thursday highest reply |
| Subject line length | 3-8 words |
| Email body length (touch 1) | 50-75 words |
| Email body length (follow-ups) | 30-50 words |

## Infrastructure health

| Metric | Red flag | Healthy |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Domain age | <30 days | >90 days |
| Warmup duration before cold send | <14 days | >21 days |
| Daily send volume per inbox | >50 | 25-40 |
| Inbox placement rate | <85% | >95% |
| Domain reputation (Google Postmaster) | Low/Medium | High |

## Campaign ROI benchmarks

| Metric | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|
| Cost per lead (meeting booked) | $50-200 depending on ACV |
| Cost per opportunity | $200-800 |
| Email sends per meeting | 150-300 |
| Campaigns to first won deal | 2-4 iterations typical |
| Time to first meeting from campaign launch | 2-4 weeks |

---

## How to use these benchmarks

1. **Health checks** — Compare campaign metrics against the "Average" column. Flag anything in "Poor".
2. **Debriefs** — Use to contextualize results. A 3% reply rate is "Good", not "low".
3. **Goal setting** — Set targets at "Good" for new campaigns, "Excellent" for optimized ones.
4. **Troubleshooting** — If a metric is "Poor", check the corresponding section:
   - Low open rate → subject lines, sender reputation, send timing
   - Low reply rate → copy relevance, ICP fit, offer strength
   - High bounce rate → list quality, verification process
   - Low meeting conversion → reply handling speed, CTA clarity
