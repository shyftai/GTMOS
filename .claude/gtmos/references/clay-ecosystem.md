## Tools worth watching

These Clay integrations could add value to GTM:OS in future versions:

1. **BuiltWith** — technographic data for ICP targeting (know what tech stack prospects use)
2. **Warmly / RB2B** — website visitor identification (know who's on your site before outreach)
3. **Datagma** — job change detection (alternative to Apollo/Crispy for job change signals)
4. **DeepL** — higher quality translation for multi-language sequences
5. **ContactOut** — strong LinkedIn email/phone finder (alternative enrichment source)
6. **Gong** — conversation intelligence (alternative to Fireflies for transcript analysis)
7. **Crunchbase** — funding signals (API key already in .env, could formalize integration)
8. **Enrow** — email finder with built-in catch-all verification
9. **La Growth Machine** — multi-channel alternative to Lemlist/Instantly
10. **Debounce** — additional email verification option for waterfall
