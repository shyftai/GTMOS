# GTM:OS Intake Interview

Structured onboarding interview. Ask one block at a time. Confirm before moving to the next block. For any field the user skips, use defaults from defaults.md.

## Block 1 — Company & Offer
- What does your company do? (one sentence)
- What's the product/service you're selling?
- Price point or deal size? (e.g., "$500/mo SaaS", "$50k ACV enterprise")
- What stage are you at? (pre-revenue, early, growth, mature)

Save to: `ICP.md` and `workspace.config.md`

## Block 2 — ICP Definition
- What industries do you target? (e.g., SaaS, fintech, healthcare)
- Company size? (employee count range, revenue range)
- Geography? (countries, regions, or "global")
- Tech stack signals? (e.g., "uses Salesforce", "runs on AWS")
- Any firmographic filters? (funding stage, hiring signals, etc.)

Save to: `ICP.md`

## Block 3 — Persona
- What titles are you targeting? (e.g., VP Sales, CTO, Head of Growth)
- Seniority level? (C-suite, VP, Director, Manager, IC)
- Department? (Sales, Marketing, Engineering, Operations)
- Who's the economic buyer vs. champion vs. user?

Save to: `PERSONA.md`

## Block 4 — Pain Points
- What are the top 3 problems your product solves?
- How are prospects currently solving these problems? (status quo)
- What's the cost of inaction? (lost revenue, wasted time, compliance risk)

Save to: `ICP.md` (pain section)

## Block 5 — Current State
- How are prospects handling this today? (manual process, competitor, nothing)
- What triggers them to look for a solution? (hiring, funding, compliance deadline)
- Common objections you hear?

Save to: `ICP.md` (current state section)

## Block 6 — Proof Points
- Best case study or customer win? (company, result, timeline)
- Key metrics you can cite? (e.g., "40% faster", "saved $200k/year")
- Any recognizable logos or social proof?

Save to: `ICP.md` (proof section)

## Block 7 — Tools
- CRM? (HubSpot, Salesforce, Attio, Pipedrive, none)
- Email platform? (Instantly, Lemlist, Smartlead, Apollo)
- Enrichment tools? (Apollo, Icypeas, Prospeo, Clay)
- Verification? (ZeroBounce, NeverBounce, MillionVerifier)
- LinkedIn? (Sales Navigator, regular, none)

Save to: `TOOLS.md`

## Block 8 — Existing Campaigns
- Have you run outbound before? (yes/no)
- What worked? What didn't?
- Any existing sequences or templates to import?
- Current reply rates, open rates if known?

Save to: `LEARNINGS.md`

## Block 9 — Infrastructure
- How many sending domains do you have?
- How many mailboxes per domain?
- Are they warmed up? (tool used, duration)
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured?

Save to: `INFRASTRUCTURE.md`

## Block 10 — Tone of Voice
- How should emails sound? (formal, casual, provocative, consultative)
- Any words or phrases to always use or never use?
- Can you paste an example email you like?

Save to: `TOV.md`

## Block 11 — CRM Pipeline
- What are your pipeline stages?
- Stage definitions? (what moves a deal forward)
- Handoff criteria from SDR to AE?

Save to: `PIPELINE.md`

## Block 12 — Booking Flow
- Calendar link? (Calendly, HubSpot, Cal.com)
- Meeting types? (intro call, demo, technical review)
- Qualification criteria before booking?

Save to: `BOOKING.md`

## Block 13 — Competitors
- Top 3 competitors?
- How do you differentiate?
- Win/loss patterns?

Save to: `COMPETITORS.md`

## Block 14 — Goals & Timeline
- Monthly meeting/demo target?
- Campaign timeline? (start date, duration)
- What does success look like in 90 days?

Save to: `ROADMAP.md`

---

After all blocks: display summary of populated files and suggest next steps based on role.
