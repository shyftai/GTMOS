## Win/Loss Interview Guide

Use this when interviewing a buyer post-close. Keep calls under 20 minutes.

**For wins:**
1. What was the primary reason you chose us over alternatives?
2. What almost made you go a different direction?
3. Who else was involved in the decision?
4. What would make you recommend us to someone else?

**For losses:**
1. Who did you choose and why?
2. What would have needed to be different for us to win?
3. Was there a feature, capability, or price point that was the deciding factor?
4. Is there a scenario where you'd revisit this decision in the future?
