# Command: Account-Based Multi-Threading

## Trigger
"Multi-thread [company]" or "Run account-based approach for [company]"

## When to use
When targeting a high-value account with multiple stakeholders. Instead of reaching one person, reach 2-4 people at the same company with role-appropriate messaging.

## Steps
1. Load ICP.md, PERSONA.md, BRIEFING.md from active workspace
2. Identify the target company and confirm it matches ICP
3. Map the buying committee — identify 2-4 contacts by role:

### Role priority
| Priority | Role | Messaging angle |
|----------|------|----------------|
| 1 | Decision maker (VP/C-suite) | Business outcome, ROI, strategic fit |
| 2 | Champion (Director/Head of) | Day-to-day pain, team impact, implementation |
| 3 | Influencer (Manager/IC) | Workflow improvement, time saved, ease of use |
| 4 | Blocker (Finance/Legal/IT) | Only if needed — security, compliance, cost |

4. Apply timing rules:
   - Stagger outreach — do NOT contact all people at the same company on the same day
   - Start with the champion (most likely to engage), then decision maker 2-3 days later
   - If champion replies positively, ask for an internal intro before reaching the decision maker cold
   - If no reply from champion after touch 2, reach the decision maker directly

5. Customize messaging per role:
   - Same campaign angle, different framing
   - Champion gets the operational pain version
   - Decision maker gets the business outcome version
   - Never copy-paste the same email to multiple people at the same company

6. Cross-reference rules:
   - If ANY contact at the company replies (positive or negative), pause all other threads
   - If one person refers you to another at the company, stop the other threads
   - Log all threads in campaign logs/decisions.md
   - Track which role converted in PIPELINE.md for future campaign learning

7. Validate against SUPPRESSION.md — check all contacts before reaching out
8. Present the multi-thread plan for approval before executing
