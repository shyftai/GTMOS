# Auto Mode Log

All decisions auto-approved in Auto execution mode are logged here with timestamp, action, and rationale.

Hard gate decisions (always requiring approval regardless of mode) are NOT logged here — they are logged in workspace-log.md after human approval.

---

## Format

```

Action: [what was auto-approved]
Context: [what triggered this decision]
Rationale: [why this passed auto-approval — no hard gates triggered]
Files modified: [if any]
```
