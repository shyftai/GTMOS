# Promotional Calendar

## Blackout rules
- No new campaign launch within 48 hours of BFCM start
- No conflicting promos within 7 days of each other (calendar conflict gate)
- No price increases during active promotional periods
- No major launches within 14 days of BFCM (audience focus splits)
- Minimum 7 days between any two major promotions

## Campaign spacing notes
- Minimum 7 days between major promotions (avoid audience fatigue)
- Maximum 2 email campaigns per week outside of BFCM season
- During BFCM: up to 2 emails per day for the 5-day BFCM window — then pause for 2 weeks
- Product launches require minimum 2-week runup
- Post-BFCM: 2-week promotional pause before holiday campaigns resume
