## Suppression segments
- Unsubscribed — never email/SMS regardless of list membership
- Hard bounced — remove and suppress permanently
- GDPR/CCPA opt-outs — region-specific suppression
- Competitors — exclude from all targeting
- Employees — exclude from acquisition campaigns
