# Agency Industry Benchmarks

Performance benchmarks for agency new business development and client retention. Load during health checks, campaign debriefs, and reporting.

Sources: industry aggregates from agency benchmarking surveys, GTM practitioner data, and campaign performance data.

---

## New business outreach benchmarks

Benchmarks for agency-to-agency-buyer outreach (you selling your services).

### Cold email

| Metric | Below average | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|--------------|---------|------|-----------|
| Open rate | < 30% | 30–38% | 38–48% | > 48% |
| Reply rate | < 3% | 3–5% | 5–8% | > 8% |
| Positive reply rate | < 1% | 1–2% | 2–3% | > 3% |
| Unsubscribe rate | > 1% | 0.5–1% | 0.2–0.5% | < 0.2% |
| Bounce rate | > 3% | 2–3% | 1–2% | < 1% |

**Why agency outreach benchmarks differ from typical B2B:**
Agency buyers (CMOs, VP Marketing) are marketing professionals. They receive more cold outreach than average. Their bar for relevance is higher. Good open rates require strong subject lines; good reply rates require specific, evidence-led copy.

### Conversion funnel

| Stage | Conversion rate | Notes |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| Positive reply → booked call | 55–70% | Move fast — respond within 4 hours |
| Booked call → proposal sent | 45–65% | Only propose if clear buying intent |
| Proposal → negotiation | 40–60% | Strong proposals with proof convert higher |
| Negotiation → closed won | 55–75% | If you're here, they want to buy |
| **Cold email → closed client** | **0.3–1%** | This is normal — volume matters |

### Pipeline velocity

| Metric | Average | Good |
|--------|---------|------|
| Cold email to first call | 14–21 days | 7–14 days |
| First call to proposal | 7–14 days | 3–7 days |
| Proposal to close | 14–30 days | 7–21 days |
| Total sales cycle | 21–45 days | 14–30 days |
| Average new client ACV | $24K–$60K/yr | $60K–$120K/yr |

### Signal-triggered benchmarks (vs. cold baseline)

| Trigger | Reply rate lift | Positive reply lift |
|---------|----------------|---------------------|
| New CMO/VP hired | 2x | 2–3x |
| Funding announced | 1.5–2x | 1.5–2x |
| Job posting signal | 1.2–1.5x | 1.2–1.5x |
| Product launch | 1.5x | 1.5x |
| No signal (cold only) | Baseline | Baseline |

## Client retention benchmarks

### Churn and retention

| Metric | Industry average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|-----------------|------|-----------|
| Annual gross churn rate | 35–55% | 20–35% | < 20% |
| 12-month retention rate | 45–65% | 65–80% | > 80% |
| Month 1–3 churn risk | Highest | — | — |
| Average agency relationship length | 14–22 months | 24–36 months | 36+ months |

**Why churn is high in the first 3 months:** Expectation gaps. The agency sold one thing; the client expected something slightly different. Onboarding and first-90-days management are the highest-leverage retention activity.

### NPS and satisfaction

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| NPS score | < 10 | 10–30 | 30–50 | > 50 |
| Client satisfaction score (1–10) | < 6 | 6–7 | 7–8.5 | > 8.5 |

### Expansion and upsell

| Metric | Average | Good |
|--------|---------|------|
| Clients that expand in 12 months | 20–30% | 30–50% |
| Upsell close rate (existing client) | 30–40% | 40–60% |
| Average expansion MRR per client | +20–30% of original MRR | +40–60% |

### Referral rates

| Metric | Average | Good |
|--------|---------|------|
| Clients who refer at least once | 20–30% | 40–60% |
| Referral intro-to-close rate | 35–50% | 50–70% |
| Referrals as % of new biz pipeline | 10–25% | 30–50% |

Referral is the highest-converting channel. If referrals are below 20% of pipeline, there's either a satisfaction problem or the ask is never being made.

## Benchmark notes

- Benchmarks vary significantly by: industry vertical, company size, ICP quality, offer strength, and copy quality
- Use these as calibration points, not hard targets — a well-targeted, well-written campaign to a hot ICP will outperform these significantly
- When results fall below average: diagnose targeting before diagnosing copy; diagnose copy before diagnosing send timing
- Update with workspace-specific data as you accumulate it — your actuals beat industry averages for forecasting
