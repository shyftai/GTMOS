## Delivery Status Definitions

- **In Progress** — being actively worked on by the team
- **In Review** — internal QA complete, pending account manager sign-off
- **Pending Approval** — sent to client, waiting on their sign-off
- **Approved** — client has confirmed acceptance
- **Done** — complete, approved, and filed in client folder
- **Blocked** — work cannot proceed — waiting on client asset, access, brief confirmation, or feedback
