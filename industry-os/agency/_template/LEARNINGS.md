# Learnings

Persistent memory for the agency. Load every session. Update after every significant event — win, loss, delivery success, client churn, upsell, QBR signal.

Before starting any new campaign, pitch, or client strategy: check this file. Do not repeat documented mistakes.

---

## New Business Learnings

Document what works and what does not when winning new clients: channels, angles, signals, personas, objections.

---

## Delivery Learnings

Document what makes delivery go smoothly and what causes friction: process issues, scope clarity, revision cycles, client communication patterns.

---

## Client Learnings

Document patterns in how clients behave, what keeps them happy, what causes them to churn or go Yellow/Red.

---

## Ops Learnings

Document internal process improvements: tooling, team workflows, capacity issues, billing patterns.

---

## Template

Copy this block when adding a new learning:

```
## [YYYY-MM-DD] [Category: New Biz / Delivery / Client / Ops]
**Context:** [what happened — brief factual summary]
**Learning:** [what this tells us about how to operate]
**Apply when / Apply to:** [specific trigger, service line, or persona type]
**Outcome:** Win / Loss / Improved / Inconclusive
```
