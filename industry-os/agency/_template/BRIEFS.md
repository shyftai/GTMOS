## Brief Quality Standards

A brief is not valid unless it has all of the following:

- **Goal** — what metric is this trying to move, and by how much?
- **Audience** — who is this for? Which persona, segment, or list?
- **Deadline** — when does the finished deliverable need to be approved?
- **Approval contact** — who on the client side signs off?
- **Scope** — what is included and what is not?

If any of these are missing, flag the brief as "In Briefing" and do not start work until they are resolved.
