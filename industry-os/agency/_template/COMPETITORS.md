## Displacement playbook

When a prospect mentions they're currently with a competitor or recently left one:

**If prospect is WITH a competitor:**
- Do not attack the competitor directly
- Ask: "What's working well with them? What would you want done differently?"
- Position as complementary or as a test on one channel first
- Full playbook: `references/agency-objections.md` — "We're happy with our current agency"

**If prospect JUST LEFT a competitor:**
- High-intent prospect — move fast
- Lead with empathy: "Most agency transitions happen for a reason — what went wrong?"
- Use their answer to differentiate specifically, not generically
- Full playbook: `references/agency-objections.md` — "We had a bad experience with an agency"
