## Roles

- **Account Manager**: client relationship, brief intake, feedback management, reporting
- **Strategist**: campaign strategy, ICP definition, positioning, competitive analysis
- **Copywriter**: email sequences, ad copy, landing pages, content
- **Media Buyer**: paid search, paid social, programmatic
- **SEO Specialist**: organic search, technical SEO, content strategy
- **Designer**: creative assets, ad creative, decks, brand work
- **Analyst**: reporting, attribution, data, dashboards

## Capacity Rules

- Max billable utilization: 80% (leave 20% for admin, sick days, internal meetings)
- New client onboarding buffer: reserve 5 hrs/week per Account Manager in the first month
- QBR prep: 2 hrs per client per quarter (block this in advance)
- New business pitch support: 4–6 hrs/week reserved for BD lead during active pitches
- Do not exceed 90% utilization for any team member — delivery quality will suffer

## Hiring Triggers

Flag for hiring when:
- Agency utilization > 80% for 2+ consecutive weeks
- MRR > $50K with fewer than 3 FTE (leverage too low)
- Closing deals is being blocked by capacity constraints
- QBR client satisfaction declining due to responsiveness issues
