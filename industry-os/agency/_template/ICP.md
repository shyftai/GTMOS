## Hiring signals — strongest indicators

These companies are most likely to need external marketing help:

- Posting for: Marketing Manager, Growth Lead, Demand Gen Manager, CMO, VP Marketing
- Just hired: First marketing hire (overwhelmed immediately), new CMO (auditing everything), VP Marketing (90-day plan window)
- Marketing team size: 1–5 people (stretched, needs execution capacity)
- Posting for roles the agency covers (e.g. paid media specialist → they want paid ads done)

## Company pain signals

- No clear attribution on ad spend — spending but can't prove ROI
- Inconsistent pipeline from marketing — spiky or flat MQLs
- Burning cash on in-house team with poor results
- New product launch with no distribution plan
- Expanding to new market or geographic segment
- Just rebranded with no campaign support
- Recently lost a key marketing hire (backfill posting = gap in execution)
- Flat or declining organic traffic with no content investment

## Buying triggers — time-sensitive

| Trigger | Window | Priority |
|---------|--------|----------|
| New CMO / VP Marketing hired | First 90 days | High |
| Series A, B, or growth round announced | First 14 days | High |
| Product launch announced | 2–4 weeks before | High |
| Multiple marketing job postings (3+) | Ongoing | Medium |
| Competitor lost a client in their space | Within 30 days | Medium |
| No marketing content in 60+ days | Evergreen | Low |

## Disqualifiers — reject these

- Under 20 employees (no budget, too chaotic, long cycle)
- Over 1,000 employees (procurement hell, 6-month sales cycles)
- **Already in CLIENTS.md** — hard stop, never contact through new business
- Pure consumer / B2C (unless e-commerce with agency track record)
- Government or regulated sector without specific compliance expertise
- Seed-stage pre-revenue (no real budget)
- Actively in an RFP process with 10+ agencies (race to bottom on price)

## ICP scoring criteria

- **Score 3 — perfect fit:** Company in target industry, 50–250 employees, active buying signal (new hire, funding, launch), has marketing team of 1–3, posts roles in services we cover
- **Score 2 — acceptable:** Right size, right industry, no active signal but pain indicators present, marketing job postings visible
- **Score 1 — borderline, manual review:** Edge on size (20–50 or 500–1000), less proven vertical, signal is weak or old
- **Score 0 — reject:** Under 20, over 1000, existing client, consumer without e-commerce, government
