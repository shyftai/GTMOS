## Health definitions

**Green** — On track, results meeting targets, client is engaged and happy. Renewal is likely.

**Yellow** — Some friction or underperformance. Could be a reporting issue, results below target, communication gaps, or a change in client-side contacts. Needs attention before it escalates. Do not start renewal conversation until yellow is resolved.

**Red** — At risk of churn. Results significantly below target, client has escalated, or there's been a breakdown in trust. Escalate immediately — account lead must be personally involved. Renewal conversation requires full results review and a recovery plan first.

## Conflict check protocol

Before starting any new business campaign or adding a prospect to a list:

1. Search this file for the prospect company name
2. Search for the prospect's domain
3. If found: HARD STOP — do not contact through new business sequences
4. If a prospect has a parent/subsidiary relationship with a current client: flag for manual review

**Suppression rule:** All current clients and recently churned clients (< 6 months) are automatically suppressed from new business outreach. Add them to SUPPRESSION.md.
