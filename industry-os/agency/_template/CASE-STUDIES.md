## Case Study: [SaaS New Business — Example]

**Industry:** B2B SaaS (HR tech)
**Company size:** 80 employees, Series A
**Challenge:** No consistent pipeline from marketing. Spending $30K/month on paid search with no attribution. CMO brought in 3 months ago and needed to show results to board.
**Approach:** Rebuilt ICP targeting and keyword strategy. Launched new LinkedIn sequence to VP HR and CPO personas. Built attribution dashboard connecting ad spend to pipeline in HubSpot.
**Result:** 3.2x pipeline increase in 90 days / CPL reduced 44% / First month attributed $280K in influenced pipeline
**Used for:** CMO persona, SaaS vertical, Performance Marketing pitches, Analytics pitches

## Case Study: [E-commerce Growth — Example]

**Industry:** D2C e-commerce (apparel)
**Company size:** $8M revenue, 35 employees
**Challenge:** Meta ROAS declining. No email automation beyond basic welcome flow. Founder doing all marketing personally and burning out.
**Approach:** Rebuilt Meta creative strategy — moved from product-led to story-led ads. Built 6-flow email lifecycle in Klaviyo. Trained founder to review weekly reports, not manage daily.
**Result:** ROAS from 1.8x to 3.4x in 60 days / Email revenue up 240% in 90 days / Founder reclaimed 15 hours/week
**Used for:** Founder persona, e-commerce vertical, Paid Social pitches, Email & Lifecycle pitches
**Available as:** Full case study / one-liner / stat only
**Client status:** Anonymized (use as "[DTC apparel brand, $8M revenue]")

## Case Study: [B2B Full-Service — Example]

**Industry:** Professional services (legal tech)
**Company size:** 120 employees, bootstrapped scaling
**Challenge:** No inbound pipeline. Sales team doing all outbound manually. No marketing hire. VP Sales was effectively CMO.
**Approach:** Built full outbound system — ICP, sequences, LinkedIn, email. Launched content program. Hired first marketing manager as part of agency scope (embedded model).
**Result:** 0 to 45 MQLs/month in 4 months / 3 enterprise deals closed from outbound in 6 months / Marketing hire placed and ramped
**Used for:** Founder persona, VP Sales persona, Full-Service Retainer pitches, B2B services vertical
**Available as:** Full case study
**Client status:** Anonymized (use as "[Legal tech company, 120 employees]")
