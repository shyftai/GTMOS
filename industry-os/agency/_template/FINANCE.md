## Billing Rules

- Net 15 for project work
- Net 30 for retainers (invoice on 1st of each month)
- Late payment: send reminder at day 7, follow-up call at day 14, pause new work at day 30
- Deposit required: 50% on all project work before work begins
- No retainer invoice without signed SOW on file

## Churn Economics

The cost to replace a churned client is approximately 3x their monthly retainer value — accounting for sales effort, onboarding time, and revenue ramp. Recovery from a churn takes 3–6 months. Prevention is 10x cheaper than replacement. This is why renewal outreach starts at 60 days, not 30.
