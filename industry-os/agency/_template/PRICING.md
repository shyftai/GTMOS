## Pricing philosophy

- Price on value and outcomes, not hours
- Never drop price on the first ask — reframe ROI first
- Three-tier structure: Starter / Growth / Scale
- Always present all three tiers — clients often self-select Growth when they see Scale
- Monthly retainer preferred over project work where possible

## Discounting rules

- First discount offer: never lower than 10% reduction for annual commitment
- Pilot/discovery sprints: acceptable at reduced scope, not reduced rate
- If prospect says "too expensive": do ROI reframe first, not price cut
- Never discount more than 20% without account director approval
- Document all discounts in COSTS.md with reason
