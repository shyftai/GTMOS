# Acceptable Use Policy

GTM:OS is designed for legitimate B2B outreach. By using this software, you agree to the following:

## Do
- Comply with all applicable anti-spam, privacy, and data protection laws
- Verify email addresses before sending
- Honour unsubscribe requests immediately
- Maintain accurate suppression lists
- Use only legitimately sourced contact data
- Include required identification and opt-out mechanisms in all messages
- Respect platform rate limits and terms of service
- Document consent where legally required (e.g., CASL)

## Do Not
- Send unsolicited commercial email in violation of applicable law
- Scrape personal data without a valid legal basis
- Bypass suppression lists or unsubscribe mechanisms
- Use the software for harassment, fraud, or deceptive practices
- Violate platform terms of service (LinkedIn, Google, Meta, etc.)
- Process sensitive personal data (health, financial, political) through outreach campaigns
- Send to personal email addresses for B2B campaigns
- Fabricate relationships, urgency, or proof points in outreach copy

## LinkedIn Automation Warning
LinkedIn explicitly prohibits automation tools. Use of GTM:OS with LinkedIn automation tools (including Crispy) is at your own risk and may result in account restrictions or permanent bans. Shyft AI is not responsible for any actions taken by LinkedIn against your account.

## Enforcement
This policy is advisory. GTM:OS is open-source software — Shyft AI cannot monitor or control how it is used. Users bear full responsibility for their compliance with applicable laws and platform policies.
