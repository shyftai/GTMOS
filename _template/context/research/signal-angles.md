# Signal Angles — [Workspace Name]

Maps detected signal types to proven outreach angles. Load this before writing signal-triggered copy.

**Updated by:** `/gtm:signals`, `/gtm:write`, manual editing after campaigns

---

## How to use

When a signal is detected for a target account, find the matching signal type below. Use the proven angle as the lead line — never reference the signal generically ("I saw you raised a round"). Reference the specific insight and connect it to the offer in 2-3 sentences.

---

## Funding signals

**Signal:** Series A / B / C announced
**Angle:** Scaling pressure — teams at this stage typically inherit tooling that wasn't built for growth
**Lead line pattern:** "Congrats on the [round] — teams usually use this moment to fix the things that were slowing them down before. [offer] is what [similar companies] sorted first."
**Expiry:** 14 days from announcement

---

## Hiring signals

**Signal:** Job post for [revenue-related role]
**Angle:** Capability gap — they're hiring for it, meaning they don't have it yet
**Lead line pattern:** "Saw you're hiring a [role] — that usually means [pain point] is on the priority list. [offer] gives [persona] a head start while the hire ramps."
**Expiry:** 30 days from post date (check if role is still open before sending)

**Signal:** New hire in [relevant role] started
**Angle:** Fresh start — new leaders audit tools and processes in their first 90 days
**Lead line pattern:** "[Name] joining as [role] is a good moment. New [role]s often look at [area] in their first quarter — [offer] is worth being on their radar early."
**Expiry:** 60 days from start date

---

## Company news signals

**Signal:** Product launch / expansion into new market
**Angle:** New surface area — new products or markets create new GTM problems
**Lead line pattern:** "Saw [company] launched [product/market]. That kind of expansion usually surfaces [specific pain] pretty fast. [offer] is how [similar companies] handled it."
**Expiry:** 21 days from announcement

**Signal:** Acquisition or merger
**Angle:** Integration chaos — systems, processes, and data don't merge cleanly
**Lead line pattern:** "Acquisitions create a lot of [area] noise. [offer] helps [persona] cut through it while the integration is still being figured out."
**Expiry:** 45 days

---

## Event signals

**Signal:** Speaker / attendee at [event]
**Angle:** Shared context — they're thinking about the problems that event addresses
**Lead line pattern:** "Saw [name] is speaking at [event] — [topic] obviously matters to [company] right now. [offer] is something worth looking at on that front."
**Expiry:** 5 days pre-event, 3 days post-event
