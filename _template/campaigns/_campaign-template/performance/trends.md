## Trend signals

After 2+ weeks of data, look for:

| Pattern | What it means | Action |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Reply rate declining week over week | Audience fatigue or list quality drop | Check if best leads were contacted first; consider pausing and refreshing list |
| Bounce rate increasing | List quality issue or domain reputation | Run email re-verification; check INFRASTRUCTURE.md |
| Opens high but replies low | Subject line works, body doesn't convert | A/B test the opening line and CTA |
| Opens declining | Subject line fatigue or deliverability drop | A/B test new subject lines; check inbox placement |
| Positive reply rate dropping | Wrong persona or angle losing relevance | Review which personas and signals convert; adjust targeting |
| Meetings from touch 3+ increasing | First touches too aggressive, later touches build trust | Consider a softer touch 1 with value-add before asking |
