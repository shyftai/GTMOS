## Testing rules

1. Test one variable at a time — never change subject AND body simultaneously
2. Minimum sample: 50 sends per variant before drawing conclusions
3. Minimum difference: 10% relative lift to call a winner
4. Run for at least 5 business days before concluding
5. Log every test, even failures — they prevent repeat mistakes
6. Apply winners to the next iteration, update copy/approved/ accordingly
7. Never test on your entire list — reserve 20% as holdout if list allows
